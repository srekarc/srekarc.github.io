Hi! I am a postdoctoral researcher in the computational biophysics group at Nagoya University, Japan. Working closely together with experimentalists, my research focuses on the computational investigation of molecular processes relevant for drug discovery. My interests include chemoinformatics/chemogenomics, molecular modeling, and data science.
